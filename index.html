Sizing preference
When on, chains are ranked by inner width first (smallest wins), then by cavity area. A narrower-but-taller chain will be preferred over a wider-but-shorter one of similar or even larger overall area. Leave off to rank purely by smallest cavity area.

Chain data

Upload your IGUS catalogue CSV or load the built-in starter set. The app will filter chains so their bend radius is ≥ the largest bend radius across all elements.

Required CSV / Excel format

First row must be exactly these column headers (works for .csv, .xlsx, and .xls):

series,inner_width_mm,inner_height_mm,bend_radius_mm,url
E4.32.050.0,50,32,48,https://www.igus.com/...
E4.32.075.0,75,32,48,https://www.igus.com/...
E4.56.100.0,100,56,75,https://www.igus.com/...
series — IGUS part series name
inner_width_mm — cavity width (Bi in IGUS drawings)
inner_height_mm — cavity height (Hi in IGUS drawings)
bend_radius_mm — chain minimum bend radius
url — product page link (optional, can be blank)
